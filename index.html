Importance of Fuel Economy

Fuel economy is a measure of how far a vehicle can travel on a specific amount of fuel. The importance of fuel economy is that it impacts the amount of money spent on gas, reduces greenhouse gas emissions by using less fuel, and positively influences the overall impact of transportation. This makes fuel economy a key factor to consider when buying a car.

Speed Management

  1. Keep your vehicle well-maintained, including regular servicing and tire pressure checks.
  2. Switch off the engine when idling for more than a minute.
  3. Plan your trips to combine errands and avoid multiple short journeys.
  4. Drive at a consistent speed, using cruise control on highways to minimize fuel consumption.
  5. Avoid carrying unnecessary weight in your car to improve fuel efficiency.
  6. Remove roof racks or cargo boxes when not in use to reduce aerodynamic drag.
  7. Carpool or use public transport whenever possible to save fuel.
  8. Consider upgrading to a fuel-efficient or hybrid vehicle if feasible.
